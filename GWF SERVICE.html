About Green Wood Services:


🏠 Showrooms
Two large showrooms you can visit:
Waterford: Unit 1C Esker Business Park,Six Cross Roads
Carlow: Unit 4 Barrow Valley Retail Park, Graiguecullen
Opening hours include weekends for convenient browsing.


🚚 Delivery & Click‑and‑Collect
They offer weekly delivery to Dublin & Cork—plus local deliveries from their Waterford & Carlow warehouses.
Click & Collect service is available, so you can order online and pick up in-store.


🛠 Assembly & Disposal
Professional assembly service is on offer (as listed under “Assembly & Disposal Charges”).
They also provide for old furniture disposal, taking away your old items for you.


💰 Finance & Returns
Finance options are available to spread payments—brands include Humm Pay & PayPal credit.
They support standard returns, consumer rights, and warranty policies (detailed in their Delivery, Returns & Consumer Rights section).


🎨 Products & Customer Care
Offer a wide variety of home‑furnishing ranges—from living and dining to bedroom and office setups, plus accessories like lamps, mirrors, rugs, etc.
Promote a family‑run ethos with friendly staff and strong attention to detail, service and value.
